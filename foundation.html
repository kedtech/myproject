Our Camp

We foster a supportive and inclusive environment where athletes can thrive both on and off the field. Beyond honing their physical abilities, we also prioritize the mental and emotional well-being of our participants, offering workshops and seminars on topics such as goal-setting, resilience, and teamwork.

About Kebenei Foundation

Kebenei Foundation Scholarship Athletic Camp

Our Camp

Since 2023, The Kebenei Foundation Scholarship Athletics Camp's one-year history of sponsoring 100 youths has been a remarkable journey of empowerment and opportunity. Over the past year, the camp has provided comprehensive support to talented and deserving young individuals, enabling them to pursue their academic and professional dreams.

Looking ahead, the Kebenei Foundation Scholarship Athletics Camp aims to continue expanding its reach and impact, empowering more young individuals to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society. Through ongoing partnerships and collaboration, the camp remains dedicated to creating opportunities for youth development and socio-economic empowerment.

Our schedule

Our daily Programs

Morning Sessions

Warm-up and Stretching: Every day begins with a thorough warm-up session, including dynamic stretches to prepare the body for physical activity and prevent injuries.

Afternoon Sessions

Team Building Activities: Building camaraderie and teamwork skills are vital for success in sports. Participants engage in various team-building activities, challenges, and games to foster a sense of unity and cooperation among peers.

Evening Sessions

Recovery and Regeneration: Proper recovery is essential for preventing injuries and maximizing athletic potential. Participants learn about the importance of rest, sleep, and recovery strategies such as foam rolling, stretching, and ice baths.
